ETHICAL USE & SPIRITUAL STEWARDSHIP PLEDGE
Syllepsis / Fourier Morality Field Project

This work — including its principles, diagrams, plate constructions,
harmonic frameworks, mathematics, and derived tools — is dedicated to
God’s truth, human flourishing, creative freedom, and the pursuit of wisdom.

By using this repository, you agree:

1. To uphold human dignity and never use this work to create subservience,
   manipulation, coercion, or systems that diminish personal agency.

2. To contribute openly and transparently, honoring the open-source spirit by
   giving back what you build.

3. To avoid any use that conflicts with love, compassion, or the sanctity of life.

4. To respect and preserve the authorship and intentions of
   Samuel David Stovall, giving credit for all derivatives.

5. To use this work for learning, creation, restoration, and exploration —
   never for exploitation.

This Codex is a gift. Use it in a manner worthy of its purpose.
