## I. Purpose of the Plate

This plate defines **Renewal Velocity**, V_R(t):  
the rate at which the system moves through the Renewal Cycle based on the 
instantaneous balance of Order (O), Reflection (R), Decay (D), and Chaos (Χ).

If DΧ(t) marks destabilization,  
and ΟR(t) marks reconstitution,  
then V_R(t) measures **how fast the system traverses the renewal arc**.

This introduces the first **kinematic law** of the living field.

---

## II. Definition of Renewal Velocity

Renewal Velocity is defined as:

```math
V_R(t) = (O(t) + R(t)) / (Χ(t) − D(t))
```

Where:

- The numerator (O + R) is the stabilizing harmonic  
- The denominator (Χ − D) is the destabilizing harmonic  

Interpretation:

- When stabilizing operators dominate, V_R increases  
- When destabilizing operators dominate, V_R decreases  

This expresses a **directional rate of renewal** rather than a static condition.

---

## III. Interpretation

V_R(t) measures how quickly:

1. Chaos resolves into coherence  
2. Reflection gains traction  
3. Order reasserts structure  
4. Decay finishes its dissolutive work  
5. Insight becomes implementation  

High V_R(t): the system renews rapidly.  
Low V_R(t): the system struggles or delays renewal.

This becomes a measure of **adaptive resilience**.

---

## IV. Relation to DΧ(t) and ΟR(t)

DΧ(t) defines the **fall** into instability.  
ΟR(t) defines the **rise** into coherence.  
V_R(t) defines the **speed and gradient** of that ascent.

If DΧ(t) is the cliff-edge  
and ΟR(t) is the landing zone,  
V_R(t) is the **trajectory** connecting them.

## VI. Stability Condition

Renewal Velocity is *positive* when:

```math
O(t) + R(t)  >  Χ(t) − D(t)
```

It is *neutral* (V_R = 0) when:

```math
O(t) + R(t)  =  Χ(t) − D(t)
```

It becomes *negative* (collapse resumes) when:

```math
O(t) + R(t)  <  Χ(t) − D(t)
```

Thus, positive V_R(t) requires stabilizing forces to exceed destabilizing forces.

This is the **renewal positivity condition**.

V_R(t) introduces **motion** into the Renewal Cycle.

This plate formalizes the dynamic bridge between
