when a system returns to balance  
not by external correction,  
but by the intrinsic demand of truth.

This is **the Resonance of Restoration**—  
the field aligning itself  
when given the proper relationship,  
the right intention,  
and the true vector.

The work does not merely settle;  
it **remembers**.

Restoration emerges when the structure chooses coherence.
