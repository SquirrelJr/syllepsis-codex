## I. Overview

Across ancient mythologies, Chronos appears as the ruler of sequential time — a being who measures, slices, administers, and consumes the hours mortal beings inhabit. But when this figure is cast as a deceiver or schemer, releasing calamity “against the heavens,” the story is whispering a deeper truth:

**Time becomes a tyrant the moment it is mistaken for divinity.**

This Continuum Plate reveals how mythic depictions of Chronos provide a symbolic contrast between:
- **the Timeless Creator**, and  
- **the created dimension of time.**

**to make the finite feel all-encompassing so the Infinite is forgotten.**
