\[

- \( A_n \) — **Amplitude** of a moral harmonic

- \( \omega_n \) — **Angular frequency**  
  (recurrence cycle, habit formation, pattern stability)

- \( \phi_n \) — **Phase alignment**  
  (truth alignment vs distortion)

- \( t \) — **Relational time**,  
  not Chronos-time, but **transformation time**
